Person-specific and within-person network estimation from intensive
longitudinal and panel data. Provides preprocessing audits,
edge-stability diagnostics, model-comparison reports, rolling forecast
validation, rolling ordinary and graphical vector autoregression,
ordinary vector autoregression (VAR), graphical vector autoregression
(graphical VAR), multilevel vector autoregression (mlVAR), native
Bayesian VAR and multilevel VAR that statistically reproduce 'Mplus'
Dynamic Structural Equation Modeling (DSEM) output without requiring
'Mplus', unified Structural Equation Modeling (uSEM), and Group
Iterative Multiple Model Estimation (GIMME) as clean-room
implementations. Split out of the 'Nestimate' package so the idiographic
time-series methods carry their own dependencies. Results have tidy
accessors and 'cograph_network' plotting support.
